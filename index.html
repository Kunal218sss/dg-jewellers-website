Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Your Vision

Based in the heart of Girgaum, Mumbai, we are a premier make-to-order jewellery atelier specializing in bespoke creations in gold, silver, platinum, and diamonds.

Our journey began in 1937, founded by my grandfather and carried forward by my father and myself. Today, the fourth generation continues this legacy of trust, craftsmanship, and timeless design.

Our Legacy

85 Years of Craftsmanship

Since 1937, DG Fine Jewels has been a trusted name in fine jewellery in Girgaum, Mumbai.

1937

What began with my grandfather's passion for craftsmanship — a small atelier in the heart of Girgaum, dedicated to creating fine jewellery that lasts generations.

1970s

The legacy was carried forward by my father, expanding our expertise to platinum and certified diamonds, becoming a household name for bridal and custom jewellery.

Today

Now in the fourth generation, we continue to specialize in fully custom-made jewellery across gold, silver, platinum and diamonds — tailored to every client's vision, budget and occasion.
